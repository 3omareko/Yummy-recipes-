Pizza Hut

Ingredients:

1- 2 teaspoons yeast.

2- Half a tablespoon of onion powder.

3- A quarter of a teaspoon of garlic powder.

4- 6 tablespoons of powdered milk.

5- 2 tablespoons of olive oil.

6- 3 cups of flour.

7- A spoonful of sugar.

8- a cup of water.

9- A spoonful of salt.

10- 2 tablespoons of cornstarch.

11- A teaspoon of baking powder.

How to prepare:

1- In a bowl, mix water, sugar, yeast, olive oil and salt until smooth Yeast reacts. 2- All the rest of the ingredients are mixed together, and the dough is kneaded for 10 minutes until the dough becomes soft and does not stick to your hands, cover it with a slightly damp cloth, put it in a warm place for 45 minutes, then divide it into 3 equal balls, and roll it out on a floured surface, then use with your favorite ingredients, Walahna Walshafa.

Italian pizza

Ingredients:

1- 2 cups of mozzarella cheese.

2- A tablespoon and a half of basil.

3- A quarter of a cup of black olives.

4 cups of pizza sauce.

5- A quarter of a teaspoon of red pepper.

6 - Onion cut into rings.

7- A cup of mushroom rings.

8- Half a cup of chopped green pepper.

9 - pizza dough.

How to prepare:

1- First, fry in a tablespoon of onion and green pepper ghee for 5 minutes on a medium heat, then add the mushrooms and fry for 5 minutes until they soften, sprinkle some salt and hot pepper, then set them aside.

2- We bring the pizza dough and put the sauce on it, then a layer of cheese and the ingredients on it, then another layer of cheese and sprinkle the basil on the face, and we put it in the oven at a medium temperature for 13 minutes until the base turns yellow and the cheese melts from the bottom, and here and on the lips.

Spicy pizza

Ingredients:

1- 2 tablespoons of olive oil.

2- A tablespoon of hot pepper.

3- A cup of water.

4- A spoonful of salt.

5- 2 tablespoons of salt.

How to prepare:

1-Mix all the ingredients and knead it well, cover the dough and leave it in a warm place for an hour and a half.

2- Knead well again on a sprinkled surface for a minute.

3- Divide the dough into 3 balls.

4- Roll out the dough in a similar way, then put it on a large greased tray And put your favorite ingredients.

5- We put it in the oven for 12 minutes on a medium heat, with our eyes and lips covered.
