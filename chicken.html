Chicken Fajita

Ingredients:

1- 1 kilo of chicken fillet (pane)

2- Salt.

3- Oil for frying.

4- Two onions, sliced.

5- a tablespoon of Nahita seasoning.

6- teaspoon red pepper.

7- Green pepper pods, sliced.

8- A spoonful of cumin.

9- a spoonful of white pepper.

How to prepare:

1- Season the chicken pieces with fajita seasoning and put them in the fridge for 60 minutes.

2- Heat the oil in a deep frying pan, then put the chicken pieces until browned, then add the onions, green peppers, and put the rest of the spices and salt, cover the pan and leave the chicken on a low heat until it is completely cooked, put in a dish Presentation, and here is the cure.

Chicken Maqluba

Ingredients:

1- 8 chicken pieces.

2- 3 bouillon cubes (dissolved in 3 liters of water).

3- Salt and black pepper.

4- 4 cups of rice.

5- A kilo of peeled eggplant (sliced).

6- Oil for frying.

7- A cup of milk.

8- Half a spoon of spices + half cinnamon + half cardamom + half turmeric (spice).

How to prepare:

1- Fry the eggplant in the hot oil until it turns brown and set aside.

2- Put the spices with the stock cubes and the chicken dissolved in water with salt and pepper, then leave them all over a low heat for 60 minutes.

3- Remove the chicken from the broth when it is soft, sprinkle two tablespoons of rice in the bottom of the heat-resistant dish, then place the chicken on top.

4 - Cover with eggplant slices, then place the rest of the rice on top of the eggplant.

5- Pour the broth over the rice until it immerses the tip of a finger over the rice.

6- Cook all for 35 minutes at 300 degrees. 7- Served with milk and nuts and placed on the face of the dish, and here and healing

6- Half a tablespoon of thyme.

7- Half a spoon of crushed bay leaf.

8- A tablespoon of flour.

9- 2 cups of ground rusk.

10- Oil for frying.

How to prepare:

1 - Mix the milk in a blender, then put the eggs.
