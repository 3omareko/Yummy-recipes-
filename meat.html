Potato tray with meat

Ingredients:

1- kilo potatoes, peeled and cut into rings.

2- a kilo of tomatoes.

3- A chopped onion.

4- Half a teaspoon of garlic.

5- a tablespoon of ghee.

6- seasoning.

7- boiled meat.

8- green pepper.

How to prepare:

1- Put the onions in a round tray with salt, black pepper and a very small amount of cumin, mix them well and put the pepper.

2- Add the ghee and stir well, then put the tomatoes after squeezing them in the bowl mixer.

3- Lay the potato rings on them, then put rings on the outside Totem for decoration.

4- Put the tray on the fire until it accommodates the potatoes.

5- Ground the meat on top of it and put it in the grill for redness, and here it is.

Marinated grilled meat

Ingredients:

1- kilo of lamb, preferably feathers.

2- A bowl.

3- A medium onion.

4- 2 tablespoons of oil.

5- a spoonful of fine cumin.

6- 3 garlic cloves.

7- A teaspoon, or as desired, hot sauce.

8- Case (bucket) of tomato sauce.

9- A cup of soy sauce.

10- spoon of black pepper.

11- a spoonful of vinegar.

12- Nutmeg, finely grated.

13- green pepper pod.

How to prepare:

1- First, wash the meat well, then beat the meat in each piece until it is spread out. After the meat is spread out, it is left to make the marinade.

Seasoning:
2- Put each of (green pepper + chili + garlic + soy + oil + vinegar + cumin + tomato + nutmeg + tomato sauce + onions) in a blender of water.

3- The ingredients are beaten until they become liquid.

4- We prepare a bowl in which we put a small amount of the marinade at the bottom, and then we start stacking the meat in layers, and between each layer and layer put the marinade liquid until it is ensured that the seasoning reaches all the meat, and leave it in the refrigerator for 200 minutes until you get the best taste, then Grill the meat, here and healing.

Moussaka with minced meat

Ingredients:

1- half a kilo of minced meat.

2- 2 large eggplant, sliced.

3-A tablespoon of finely chopped garlic.

4- Oil for greasing.

5- Salt and black pepper.

6- 2 tablespoons of flour.

7- A large glass of tomato juice.

8- A tablespoon of red vinegar.

9- Two medium onions, cut into small pieces.

10- Tablespoon of tomato sauce.

11- Parsley for garnish.

Cheese Sauce Ingredients:
1- A little butter.

2- A pinch of grated nutmeg.

3- scrambled eggs.

4- teaspoon mustard.

5- a small cup of grated cheese.

6- A cup of milk.

7- Salt.

Preparation method:

1- Put salt on the eggplant and leave for 30 minutes, heat the meat in a pot over a low heat until the fat melts, stir until the meat is red, then get rid of any excess fat, then put onions, garlic and flour with stirring and add tomato sauce and vinegar, stirring constantly, then add tomatoes, salt and pepper, cover the pot and leave it until it is completely set.

2- After that, the eggplant is rinsed and dried, then grilled on the grill until the eggplant is browned.

3- The butter is heated, we prepare the cheese sauce, then add the flour and leave it until it is cooked, taking into account the stirring, then add the cheese, nutmeg, mustard, salt and pepper, leave the sauce to calm down, then add the egg.

4- We prepare a tray with half the amount of meat and half the amount of eggplant on it, and we repeat until the meat and eggplant are finished, after that we put the cheese sauce on it and put it in the oven until it turns red. And felicitously and labrum.
